About us

Who Am I ?

Hi, I'm Alban Chatelet and I'm in my 1st year of BUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet professions) in Montbéliard.

This website is a project based on the line "I want to play a game". Its aim is to present various articles about the "Saw" horror film franchise.

So the theme of this site is 'Saw' and everything that surrounds it, including the director, a book & music associated with it.

Glossary

Jigsaw : is a fictional character who is both the central character and main antagonist in the Saw series of horror films created by James Wan and Leigh Whannell.

Trap percussion : Trap (a sub-genre of Hip Hop) is a musical style that originated in the Dirty South and emerged in the early 2000s in the southern United States.

Horrorcore : Musical genre mixing hip-hop based on themes and lyrics from horror films.

Sample : Musical sample.

Body horror : Sub-genre of horror that intentionally exposes graphic or psychologically disturbing violations of the human body.
